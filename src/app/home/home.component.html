Having a personal shitcoin might feel great.

However, it does not provide much value to the world.

Without utility, your shitcoin is just some code on a blockchain.

To prove how easy it is to create a BEP20 shitcoin, we made this website, where you can create your own with just a few clicks.

Since our coins are created according to the BEP20 token standard, you can import them in any wallet that supports it.

The shitcoin's owner will be your wallet address that you used to connect to our site.

Every coin will be transferred to your address.

Most of the costs of creating a shitcoin will be the gas you will pay to deploy your shitcoin to the blockchain.

Type in your shitcoin's name, symbol and total supply, then click Mint.

That's it, you have a worthless shitcoin.
